  • IPipeFitting

    Pipe Fitting Interface.

    An IPipeFitting can be connected to other IPipeFittings, forming a Pipeline. IPipeMessages are written to one end of a Pipeline by some client code. The messages are then transfered in synchronous fashion from one fitting to the next.

  • IPipeMessage

    Pipe Message Interface.

    IPipeMessages are objects written intoto a Pipeline, composed of IPipeFittings. The message is passed from one fitting to the next in syncrhonous fashion.

    Depending on type, messages may be handled differently by the fittings.
